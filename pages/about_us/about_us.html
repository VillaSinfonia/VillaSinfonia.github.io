The Villa Sinfonia Foundation had its beginnings in 1975 when Lynn and Roy Oakley started the Oakley Violin Studio, a private violin studio. Since that time, the private studio has expanded to include other private violin, viola and cello studios in a 501 (c)3 non-profit organization called the Villa Sinfonia Foundation, founded in 1994.

Awards and Achievements

  1. Gold Award winners at the Pacific Basin Music Festival, Hawaii, 1994.
  2. Governor’s Award from the San Francisco Chapter of the Recording Academy’s Grammy Association, 2001.
  3. Certificate of Honor from the Board of Supervisors for the City and County of San Francisco for 25 years of outstanding service to the community, 2001.
  4. Certificate of Special Congressional Recognition from Nancy Pelosi, 2008
  5. Certificate of Appreciation from the San Francisco Police Department in gratitude for “your incredible rendition of the National Anthem at the 10th Annual law Enforcement Appreciation Night at AT & T Park, July 23, 2013”
